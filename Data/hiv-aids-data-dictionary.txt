COLUMN NAME	FORMAT	DEFINITION
Year	String	"Indicates year of HIV/AIDS diagnosis, year of death, or year living for tables 1) Newly diagnosed HIV/AIDS cases   2) Death of persons with HIV/AIDS infection, and 3) Persons living with HIV/AIDS, respectively. "
Category	String	"Indicates the general demographic categories (Age (years), Transmission category (Primary Risk encountered), Race/Ethnicity, and Gender) under which the HIV/AIDS indicators are broken down into specific groups (e.g. White, Black, etc). Values are mutually exclusive within each demographic category."
Group	String	"Indicates sub divisions of the HIV_AIDS category.
Gender category: Gender is determined by both the current gender and sex at birth data fields.  Transgender values are assigned when current gender is identified as ""Transgender"" or a discrepancy is identified between a person's sex at birth and their current gender (e.g. cases where sex at birth is ""Male"" and current gender is ""Female"" will become Transgender: Male to Female.) 
Gender values: Female, Male, Transgender: Female To Male, Transgender: Male To Female

Race/Ethnicity category: Prior to 2003, Asian and Native Hawaiian/Pacific Islanders were classified as one group. Thus, in order to present these race/ethnicity categories separately, living cases recorded under this combined classification were redistributed according to their expected proportional population representation estimated from  post-2003 data.
Race/Ethnicity values: American Indian/Alaskan Native, Asian, Black/African American, Hispanic/Latino, Native Hawaiian/Pacific Islander, White, Multiple races, Unknown race

Age Group (years): For newly diagnosed, age of persons at diagnosis.  For living cases, age of persons at year end for the reported year.  For deaths, age of person at time of death.
Age Group (years) values: 0-11, 12-14, 15-17, 18-19, 20-24, 25-29, 30-34, 35-39, 40-44, 45-49, 50-54, 55-59, 60-64, 65-74, 75 and over

Transmission category: Represents category of highest reported exposure risk. MSM = Men who have sex with men; IDU = Intravenous drug users; MSM/IDU = Men who have sex with men who are also intravenous drug users; Heterosexual (HRH)= Heterosexual contact with a high risk partner (HIV infected partner or a partner who is an IDU or MSM); Non-High Risk Heterosexual (Non-HRH) = cases with no other identified risk who reported engaging in heterosexual intercourse with a person opposite sex of their sex-at-birth.
Perinatal = children and adults exposed perinatally (HIV-positive mother);  Other = child and adult cases with hemophilia, blood transfusion, or occupational exposures; Unknown = includes missing/unknown/no reported risk information. "
Count	Numeric	"For table ""Newly diagnosed HIV/AIDS cases,"" this is the count of persons who were living in California when they were diagnosed with HIV during the reported year. For the table  ""Persons living with HIV/AIDS,"" this is the count of persons living with an HIV/AIDS diagnosis whose most current known residence at the end of the reported year was in California.  For tables ""Death of persons with HIV/AIDS infection,"" this is the count of persons living with an HIV/AIDS diagnosis whose most current known residence at the time of death during the reported year was in California."
